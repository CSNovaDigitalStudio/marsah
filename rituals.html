The Art of Scent

Rituals

Fragrance becomes meaningful when it enters the rhythm of everyday life.

Morning to evening

Compose the atmosphere.

Begin with a diffuser as the quiet foundation. Add a candle when the room slows down. Wear a personal fragrance as the final, intimate layer.

01

Set the tone

Place reeds in the bottle and allow the fragrance to settle gradually into the room.

02

Layer with light

Burn a candle for moments of pause, conversation or evening reflection.

03

Carry the memory

Choose a personal scent or car diffuser to let the fragrance follow your day.

Care

Created to be experienced slowly.

Keep fragrance away from direct sunlight and excessive heat. Rotate diffuser reeds only when a stronger scent is desired. Trim candle wicks before each burn for a cleaner flame.
